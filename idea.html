Creative Idea: Shared Internet Resource Platform for Startups and SMEs

In the context of Vietnam promoting innovation and digital transformation, tens of thousands of startups and small businesses are launched every year. These entities play a pioneering role in experimenting with new ideas, technologies, and business models. However, a major barrier they face is the cost of accessing Internet infrastructure and digital resources.

Proposed Solution: Develop a shared Internet resource platform operating under the model of "Infrastructure as a Service" (IaaS), specifically serving Vietnam's startup and SME ecosystem.

Main Components:

  • Flexible Subdomain Repository under ".vn": Startups can register subdomains like startupA.vnzone.vn, ensuring professionalism and national digital sovereignty.
  • Dynamic IP and Virtualized Networking (ASN): Provide shared virtual networking with logical routing to host digital services without complex setups or high costs.
  • Optimized Internal DNS Services: Using DNS caching, DNS over HTTPS, and VNIX local routing to enhance security and reduce latency for domestic users.
  • Local Cloud with AI & Big Data Support: Enable low-cost deployment of data analytics, machine learning tools, and AI APIs like GPT, Computer Vision, etc.
  • Centralized AI-assisted Dashboard: An intuitive web interface with virtual assistant to guide domain setup, monitor performance, and optimize network usage.

Expected Benefits:

  • Lower entry barriers for thousands of small businesses, student teams, NGOs, and startups to adopt digital infrastructure.
  • Efficiently utilize national Internet assets (e.g. .vn domains, VNIX, national IP blocks) to prevent waste or misuse.
  • Encourage digital sovereignty within Vietnam’s startup community, avoiding dependency on foreign platforms (AWS, Google Cloud).
  • Boost nationwide innovation by providing a fast, low-cost, and trustworthy testbed for emerging ideas.

Deployment Roadmap:

  • Cooperate with VNNIC, Ministry of Information & Communications, and local ISPs.
  • Operate under a nonprofit tech organization or social-impact startup.
  • Offer free access for 1–2 years to certified startups from incubators or official entrepreneurship programs.
Vision: If successful, this platform could become a national "Digital Booster Hub," where everyone—startups, students, small businesses—can join the innovation race and contribute to Vietnam’s digital future by 2045.
